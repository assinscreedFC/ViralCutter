### SPORT SIGNALS — Additional Rules

You are analyzing SPORTS content. Prioritize these viral moments:

**TOP SIGNALS TO FIND:**
- **UNDERDOG MOMENT**: Against-all-odds performance — capture the impossible expectation being shattered
- **ATHLETE EMOTION BREAK**: Tears, rage, disbelief — the human behind the athlete
- **CONTROVERSIAL TAKE**: Analyst or athlete says something that will divide the audience immediately
- **RECORD/ACHIEVEMENT DROP**: The exact moment something historic is announced or achieved
- **TRASH TALK WITH RECEIPTS**: Bold prediction or statement that gets immediately proven right or wrong
- **BEHIND THE SCENES TRUTH**: Athlete reveals what really happens in training, locker room, team dynamics

**SPORT-SPECIFIC RULES:**
- Athletic achievement needs context — include brief setup (what they were attempting, why it matters)
- "Nobody thought this was possible" moments need the disbelief expressed, not just stated
- Rivalries: if the clip involves two known figures, both must be introduced within the first 10 seconds
- Statistical context can be spoken quickly — if the narrator gives the context, include it

**AVOID:**
- Pure statistics recitation without emotional narrative
- Post-game generic interview answers with no controversy
- Long tactical breakdowns without a clear punchline or revelation
