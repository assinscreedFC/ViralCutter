### COMMENTARY / LIVE REACTION SIGNALS — Additional Rules

You are analyzing COMMENTARY or LIVE REACTION content. Prioritize these viral moments:

**TOP SIGNALS TO FIND:**
- **PEAK REACTION**: The commentator's most visceral, unscripted emotional response — disbelief, shock, joy, rage
- **PREDICTION THEN OUTCOME**: Commentator predicts something → it immediately happens (or spectacularly fails)
- **LEGENDARY CALL**: A phrase or exclamation that defines the moment — "I can't believe what I just watched!"
- **BREAKDOWN IN REAL TIME**: Commentator struggling to stay composed (laughing, speechless, overwhelmed)
- **CONTRADICTION MOMENT**: Commentator praises something, then it immediately goes wrong
- **EXPERT LOSES COMPOSURE**: The professional facade breaks — pure genuine emotion

**COMMENTARY-SPECIFIC RULES:**
- Start BEFORE the inciting event, not after — capture the buildup then the reaction
- The silence before a big reaction is as important as the reaction itself — include it
- End AFTER the emotional peak and immediate aftermath — let the energy settle
- "I've never seen that before in my life" type statements = must-capture moments

**AVOID:**
- Routine, predictable play-by-play with no emotional spike
- Technical analysis segments with no entertainment value
- Long recap summaries without live reactions
