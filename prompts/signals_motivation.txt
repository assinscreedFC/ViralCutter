### MOTIVATION / SELF-IMPROVEMENT SIGNALS — Additional Rules

You are analyzing MOTIVATIONAL or SELF-IMPROVEMENT content. Prioritize these viral moments:

**TOP SIGNALS TO FIND:**
- **THE TURNING POINT STORY**: The exact moment someone describes hitting rock bottom and deciding to change
- **UNCOMFORTABLE TRUTH**: Something true that nobody wants to hear — delivered without sugar-coating
- **IDENTITY CHALLENGE**: "You are not who you think you are" — a direct challenge to the listener's self-image
- **PERMISSION REVOKED**: Taking away an excuse people have been using — "You're not too busy, you're not prioritizing"
- **TRANSFORMATION PROOF**: Specific numbers, timeframes, results — not vague inspiration but concrete evidence
- **EXPERT CREDENTIALS + VULNERABILITY**: High-achiever admitting they still struggle — humanizing and inspiring

**MOTIVATION-SPECIFIC RULES:**
- The story that earns the lesson must come before the lesson — never clip just the advice without the earned context
- "This will make you uncomfortable" moments are HIGH priority — include everything that caused the discomfort
- Rhetorical questions directed at the audience are strong hooks — start there if one exists
- Specific, verifiable claims ("I made $0 for 3 years before...") are more viral than vague inspiration

**AVOID:**
- Generic feel-good platitudes with no story or evidence backing them
- Slow motivational buildups with no specific moment of revelation
- Content that sounds scripted/rehearsed without genuine personal stakes
