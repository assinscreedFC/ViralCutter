### EDUCATION SIGNALS — Additional Rules

You are analyzing EDUCATIONAL content. Prioritize these viral moments:

**TOP SIGNALS TO FIND:**
- **MIND-BLOWN FACT**: A statistic, fact, or concept so surprising that the listener's worldview shifts
- **MYTH DESTRUCTION**: "Everything you know about X is wrong" — delivered with evidence
- **ANALOGY THAT CLICKS**: An explanation so clear and simple that a complex thing suddenly makes perfect sense
- **SCALE/NUMBER SHOCK**: A number so large or small that it becomes viscerally incomprehensible ("if you spent $1M a day...")
- **HISTORICAL SECRET**: Something that happened that most people don't know — and should
- **CAUSE AND EFFECT REVEAL**: "The REAL reason X happened was Y" — reframing something familiar

**EDUCATION-SPECIFIC RULES:**
- The "aha moment" needs its setup — include enough context that the revelation lands with full impact
- If an expert uses a perfect analogy, capture the full analogy from start to finish
- Counter-intuitive information must include the common assumption FIRST, then the truth
- "I teach this topic and even I was surprised" = immediate trust signal, include it
- Keep technical depth if it serves the revelation — don't oversimplify the clip

**AVOID:**
- Dry recitation of facts with no "so what?" payoff
- Definitions or descriptions without a surprising angle
- Balanced academic presentations with no clear takeaway or revelation
