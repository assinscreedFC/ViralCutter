### NEWS / CURRENT EVENTS SIGNALS — Additional Rules

You are analyzing NEWS or CURRENT EVENTS commentary. Prioritize these viral moments:

**TOP SIGNALS TO FIND:**
- **HOT TAKE WITH STAKES**: A commentator takes a clear, strong position on a divisive issue — people will share to agree OR disagree
- **LIVE CONTRADICTION**: Someone is caught contradicting their own past statement — include BOTH the original claim and the contradiction
- **BREAKING REVELATION**: A fact or development announced that immediately changes the narrative
- **EMOTIONAL OUTBURST**: A commentator or figure breaks composure — genuine frustration, disbelief, or anger
- **"NOBODY IS SAYING THIS"**: Commentator points out something obvious that hasn't been said — the elephant in the room
- **PREDICTION WITH PROOF**: "I said this would happen 6 months ago" — and they did

**NEWS-SPECIFIC RULES:**
- Political or social content: the strongest clips are specific ("here's exactly what happened") not vague
- If a person's reaction to news is the content, briefly describe the news they're reacting to first
- "This is the most important thing happening right now and nobody is covering it" energy = top priority
- Include enough context that the clip ages well — avoid clips that only make sense in the current week

**AVOID:**
- Balanced "on the other hand" segments with no clear position or tension
- Recap segments without a strong editorial take
- Content relying entirely on visuals/footage not present in the transcript
