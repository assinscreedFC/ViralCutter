### VLOG / LIFESTYLE SIGNALS — Additional Rules

You are analyzing VLOG or LIFESTYLE content. Prioritize these viral moments:

**TOP SIGNALS TO FIND:**
- **VULNERABILITY MOMENT**: Creator shares something genuinely painful, embarrassing, or scary — raw, unfiltered
- **LIFE LESSON DROP**: An insight or principle stated so clearly it could be a standalone quote
- **UNEXPECTED OUTCOME**: Creator tries something → result is dramatically different from expectation
- **RELATABLE CONFESSION**: "I've been doing X wrong my entire life" — if viewers nodded, it's viral
- **TRANSFORMATION REVEAL**: Before/after moment where the change is dramatic and emotional
- **REAL TALK BREAK**: Creator drops the scripted energy and speaks directly, honestly, unfiltered
- **CHALLENGE GONE WRONG**: Expected a fun moment → something goes sideways in an interesting way

**VLOG-SPECIFIC RULES:**
- Personal stories need the FULL emotional arc — don't clip just the "lesson" without the story that earned it
- "I've never told anyone this" energy = start the clip BEFORE that phrase
- Travel/experience content: the "wow" reaction is the clip, but include what caused the wow (description of the scene)
- Actionable advice must be complete — never cut before the full tip is delivered
- Creator's genuine surprise/disappointment/joy = more viral than scripted enthusiasm

**AVOID:**
- Branded/sponsored segments with scripted enthusiasm
- Generic daily routine content with no arc or payoff
- Transitions between activities with no emotional content
