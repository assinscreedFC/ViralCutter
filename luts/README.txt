Place .cube LUT files in this directory.

The color_grading.py script expects these filenames by default:
  cinematic.cube
  vintage.cube
  warm.cube
  cool.cube
  contrast.cube

You can also pass any custom .cube filename directly to apply_lut().

Free LUT packs available at:
  - SmallHD free LUTs       https://www.smallhd.com/community/free-luts
  - Lutify.me free pack      https://lutify.me/free-lut/
  - RocketStock 35 free LUTs https://www.rocketstock.com/free-after-effects-templates/35-free-luts-for-color-grading-videos/
  - FreshLUTs                https://freshluts.com
  - Ground Control           https://groundcontrolcolor.com/pages/free-luts
